DPSG MUN 2018

All India opposition Parties Political Meet(AIOPPM)

Agenda: “Formulating a roadmap for the 2019 General Elections with special emphasis on Mahagathbandhan.”

Indian Politics is the zenith of volatile arguments and epitome of robust dealings with the cultural diversity of the Indian State. Post independence,politics was dominated by the Indian National Congess with almost no opposition till the late 70's. Then came the era where the INC started losing its omnipresence in the Indian political scenario, and the nation witnessed the upsurge of political parties who became a part of the formidable opposition. Now the time has come, when the 'Lotus' is taking over the Indian states one by one and the INC which once seemed image result for undefeatable, currently finds itself at the helm of affairs but on side opposition. This committee sets its objective as foreseeing the future of the current utopian 'Mahagatbandhan', which can be defined as the coming together of the political parties and its personas, with a unanimous motive of uprooting the 'Lotus' and putting an end to the magnanimous reign of Shri Narendra Damodardas Modi. The committee will consist of all the political parties except the Bharatiya Janata Party, and the participating members are expected to lay a roadmap while nullifying their age old political and idealistic differences to finally achieve the goal of 'Mahagatbandhan' . Presenting to you, the fifth committee of DPSG MUN 2018, All India Opposition Political Parties Meet.
