Message

Mrs Chandralekha Rawat

Principal

Dear Friends, Greetings!
Let me start by formally inviting and welcoming tomorrow’s leaders to today’s Model United Nations! We, at DPS Guwahati, have always welcomed avenues that would allow our young, dynamic leaders to widen their horizons beyond mere textbook knowledge and venture into the less explored territories of International Politics and Policy Making. With this backdrop, I on behalf of the organising committee request your enthusiastic participation in the 3rd Edition of DPSGMUN Conference slated from 23rd to 25th Nov 2018.
The global order today is undergoing tremendous transformation and we citizens are actively playing our own part. Today, we live in a world that is rife with conflict and strife amongst nations. Wars – whether overt or covert – divert the already scarce resources away from any nation’s developmental goals into unproductive, wasteful channels with no fruitful outcome. The need of the hour is to bring about the youth to a common forum, where the conflicts can be resolved through engagement, dialogue and mutual understanding.
The DPS Guwahati Model United Nations is built on open minds and fresh ideas. Besides many daunting international or national issues delegates will also draw focus and deliberate on NRC and the Citizenship Amendment Bill and their ramifications on Assam. Delegates can be the real catalysts for change by creating social networks and as leaders of the future, you can come up with innovative approaches to help confront the challenges facing the global community.
We are happy that the United Nations is also celebrating 100 years of Dr Nelson Mandela and through this platform the committee of the African Union will share and cherish the philosophy of this global leader. Through DPSGMUN 2018, where eight committees will be simulated we hope to provide a unique opportunity to engage students in more productive dialogues, while developing strong friendship bonds. The focus will be on concrete solutions and ways to make our world a better and more secure place to live.
Dear young leaders, I thank you all for your commitment and wish you a stimulating conference.
With best wishes,
Mrs Chandralekha Rawat
Principal
Delhi Public School Guwahati

DPSG MUN 2018

ABOUT:

DPS Guwahati ventures into its premier experience as a host for an Inter Model United Nations conference this November 2018 from 23rd to the 25th. The conference comprises of Eight committees namely UNHRC, HUNEP, UNSC, AIOPPM, UNCA, ALA,UNODC-IOC and African Union. Encompassing an array of diverse agendas, DPSG MUN 2018 hopes to inculcate the interests of each and every participant it serves as a host to, most vehemently, that every opinion, reason and voice raised and discussed shall surely make a change in the global scenario, if not right at the moment, then in the near future. The institution takes great pride to introduce DPS Guwahati's third ever venture into the MUNing platform, and is confident that this conference will not only result in the shaping up of future ambassadors and leaders for a positive change, but also hone a whole new generation capable of creating a difference using their cognitive and oratorical skills, not only by mediums of holding in posts, but largely by being a part of a public who is well versed in the global political, economic, social and other scenarios
